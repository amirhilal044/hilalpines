ABOUT

Established in 1970, our family business is a testament to generations of commitment to excellence and a passion for delivering premium quality nuts. Specializing in Lebanese pine nuts, we have expanded our offerings to include a diverse range of nuts such as almonds, walnuts, pistachios, raisins, cranberries, pecans, and more. Additionally, we take pride in providing a variety of honey and its related products. With a rich history spanning over five decades, we have become synonymous with unrivaled quality and a dedication to sourcing the finest ingredients.

Lebanese Pine Nuts: A Distinctive Offering:

    Our flagship product, Lebanese pine nuts, holds a special place in our assortment. Renowned for their superior quality, they stand out for several reasons:

    1. Premium Quality Origins: Sourced from Lebanon, our pine nuts benefit from the country's unique climate and soil conditions, resulting in a flavor profile that surpasses pine nuts from other regions.

    2. Health Benefits: Lebanese pine nuts are not only prized for their rich, buttery taste but also for their health benefits. Packed with nutrients, these nuts are an excellent source of antioxidants, healthy fats, and essential minerals. They contribute to heart health, boost energy, and support overall well-being.

    3. Sustainable Harvesting: Our commitment to sustainability is reflected in our sourcing practices. The Lebanese pine nuts are harvested responsibly, ensuring the preservation of the region's natural ecosystem.
